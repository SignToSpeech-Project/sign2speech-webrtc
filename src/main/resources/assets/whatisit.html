Sign2Speech was imagined by Edwin NIOGRET, Matthieu NOGUERON and Reatha TITH, all Computer Science students from Polytech Grenoble engineering school. It was developed for our end-of-fourth-year-project.

Our idea was to make communication between a mute/signing person and someone who doesn't know the sign language easier. Not many people know the sign language and Sign2Speech aims to break this barrier.

To do so, we are using Intel's Real Sense camera for gesture recognition. You can use this web application to chat with a friend if one of you knows the sign language and the other doesn't. The camera will recognize the gestures made and translate them. Their meaning will be sent in real-time as subtitles on the video and you will both be able to communicate easily.
